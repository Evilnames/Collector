

3. Beekeeping
Why it fits: Passive placement system (like bird feeders/baths) that creates synergies with existing systems — farming yield boost, wildflower bonus, and feeds directly into mead production.

Hive placement: Place bee boxes in world. Bees passively forage nearby flowers + farm crops
Biome honey: Honey type/flavor derived from surrounding wildflower biome
Tropical biome → passion fruit honey
Alpine → wildflower mountain honey
Wetland → clover honey
Synergy effects:
Crops within hive radius get +10–20% yield (pollination)
Wildflowers near hive respawn faster
Harvest: Collect honeycomb → extract honey + beeswax
Mead production: Honey + water → ferment (reuses wine fermentation pattern) → aged mead
Mead flavors inherit honey biome profile
Sparkling mead variant (like sparkling wine)
Beeswax uses: Candles (light source + ambient buff), wood polish (enhances tool durability), wax seals for wine bottles (cosmetic bonus)
Research: Apiary Basics → Honey Harvest → Pollination Arts → Master Apiarist (4 nodes)
Files: bees.py, UI/bees.py, extend blocks.py, items.py, farming integration





Tier 3 — Flavor & World-Building





Seashells
A pure collection system for coastal/ocean biomes. Shells have shape, color, pattern, and condition — similar complexity to rocks but lighter. Used as inputs for jewelry and decoration.

Aquarium / Terrarium
Functional display blocks where caught fish and insects can be placed and observed. Adds a "living collection" dimension and gives fish/insects a home-base presence.



Dendrology
Study tree species. Old-growth trees reveal ring cross-sections when cut (age, climate history). Heritage trees in rare biomes are single specimens. Wood grain and density varies by species, feeding into better tool handles, instrument bodies, or charcoal quality.



Crafting Branches
Glassblowing
Sand (biome-variant) + heat → blown glass. Vessels for spirits/wine that add aging bonuses. Stained glass from gemstone dust. Display cases, aquarium tanks, specimen jars all require glass. Unlocks aesthetics for the base.


Tracking & Sign Reading
Animal tracks, scat, and feeding signs spawn in mud/snow/dirt. Following a trail leads to a den or feeding ground. Some rare animals (large elk, rare boar) only encountered via tracking — can't be found by wandering. Adds a new interaction verb to the world.

Tidal Zones / Ocean Depth
Tiered ocean exploration: shoreline → reef → twilight zone → deep. Each zone has distinct fish, corals, seashells, bioluminescent creatures. Depth requires gear upgrades. Reef zones have coral gardening (fragment → grow → harvest).



Fossil Excavation Lab
A grid-based preparation mini-game. The fossil is embedded in a matrix — you choose tool (pick, brush, air scribe) per cell. Too much force cracks the specimen; too little and matrix remains. Reveals the fossil layer by layer like a spatial puzzle. Final quality scored on intact cells.


Timing / Precision
Insect Netting
Insects move in erratic paths. You sneak closer — move too fast and it flies. When in range a net-swing targeting reticle appears; mistimed swing and it escapes. Rarer species require longer approach chains.

Glassblowing
Rotate the blowpipe (left/right keys), blow at timed intervals to expand the bubble, shape it before it cools. Asymmetry = flawed vessel. Nailing the shape determines vessel quality tier.

Fly Casting / Fishing Variant
A casting mini-game where current lines are visible. You load the cast (power bar), release to land in a feeding lane. Fish in different lanes require different presentations. The existing fishing mechanic handles the bite; this is the setup layer.




Skill Expression
Archery / Hunting
Wind direction indicator + trajectory arc. You aim, the arc drifts with wind, you release. Follow-up tracking brings you to the downed animal or lost arrow. Creates a full loop: stalk → aim → track.

Tracking Puzzle
A sequence of environmental signs (track impressions, bent grass, fur snag, scat) laid across a small area. You read them in order and deduce: species, direction, time since passage (fresh/old), behavior (fleeing/foraging). Correct deduction leads you to the animal or den.
